The Untold Story

Montana Didn't Wait
for Appalachia

Montana went dry in 1916 — two years before the rest of the nation. Then it walked it back in 1929, before anyone else. Lady bootleggers ran stills in Glacier Park. The Bootlegger Trail ran whiskey from Canada through open range. This is Montana's moonshine story. Nobody's been telling it.
