Montana-Born, Handcrafted with Grit

The Brand

Born in Montana,
Made with Grit

Montucky Moonshine brings you handcrafted products born from great ideas and a passion for quality. From custom-designed mugs and steins to bold graphic tees and unique merchandise — every item carries the spirit of Montana craftsmanship.

What started as a bold idea in Helena has grown into a brand that represents authentic Montana living. Every product is designed with the same rugged spirit that built the state.

Milestones

The Journey

The Beginning

An Idea Takes Shape

Born from a passion for Montana craftsmanship and authentic goods, Montucky Moonshine started as a vision to create products that embody the spirit of the state.

Building the Brand

First Products Launch

The first collection of mugs, steins, and merchandise hit the Shopify store. Each piece hand-selected and designed to represent Montana authenticity.

The Venue

A Space is Born

The private venue at 3420 Byron Road comes to life — hand-built from the ground up. Every plank, every sign, every mason jar placed with intention.

Growing
