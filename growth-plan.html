Task 10

SEO & Schema Markup

The site is invisible to Google for every commercial keyword. Zero indexed product pages, broken URLs, no schema — here's the full remediation list.

LocalBusiness Schema — index.html
JSON-LD with name, address, email, sameAs (Facebook). Already live on the proposal site.
Product Schema — product.html
Full Product schema with aggregateRating — enables star ratings in Google search results. Live on proposal site product pages.
Canonical Tags — all pages
rel="canonical" present on all pages pointing to montuckymoonshine.com. Prevents duplicate content penalties.
Shopify — Fix 404 product URLs
Previously indexed URLs for hats, hoodies, aging sticks now return 404. Need 301 redirects or product restoration in Shopify admin → Online Store → Navigation → URL redirects.
Meta Descriptions — all 15 products
All product pages have auto-generated or blank meta descriptions. Update each in Shopify admin → Products → [Product] → Search engine listing preview. Target 120–160 chars with "moonshine gifts" / "Montana merchandise" keywords.
Google Business Profile
3420 Byron Road Helena MT not claimed on Google. Zero presence for "Helena distillery" or "Montana gift shop" local searches. Claim at business.google.com — takes 15 minutes, drives immediate local visibility.
Blog / Content Strategy
No content exists. Target: "moonshine cocktail recipes" (high vol.), "Montana gifts" (commercial intent), "what is moonshine" (educational). 2 posts/month for 6 months = first-page results for 4–6 keywords.
Sitemap to Google Search Console
Submit montuckymoonshine.com/sitemap.xml to Search Console. Monitor for crawl errors, index coverage, and Core Web Vitals.
